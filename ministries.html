Sunday Mornings

"On Sunday mornings, we meet at 9:45a to pray together. Our Sunday morning service begins at 10:30a, where we sing, read the Word of God, receive offerings, and receive the preached Word. After the morning service we enjoy a meal together (all are welcome!) . We conclude our day with an afternoon service to follow. A nursery is provided for infants through 2 years old.

Wendesday Evenings

Our Wednesday evenings resemble a “Sunday School,” in that we meet together in the sanctuary to sing, and then are dismissed to age-divided classes. The Sprouts are Pre-K to about 1st grade. Our Truth Seekers class is from about 2nd grade to 6th grade. Our Vision Youth class is for 7th-12th grade. Each class is taught by a couple, and the age divisions have some flexibility based upon the need of the child and discretion of parents/guardians.

Events

We have Ladies Breakfast on the first Saturday of each month, and Men’s Breakfast on the 3rd Saturday of each month. The focus of these times is fellowship and Bible Study specificallly for the needs of men and women. We also have Youth Nights which are scheduled periodically, which feature refreshments, games, and a Bible message.
