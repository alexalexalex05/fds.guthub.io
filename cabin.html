Elisha Cabin

"The Elisha Cabin is a small cottage built and designed to be a help to those in full-time ministry for the Lord. The Cabin is a 24’x24” buildind designed for year-round usage. There is a galley-style kitched, a full bathroom, two bedrooms, and a dining/living/common area. There is no laundry available at this time, but there are local laundromats available. Reservations are limited to two weeks and are on a first-come, first serve basis. Please contact our church at 207-466-1611 or email us at southlibertybaptist@gmail.com to make arrangements."
