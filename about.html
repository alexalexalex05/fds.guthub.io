Our Beliefs

WHAT DO WE DO?

The purpose and mission of the church can be summed up in Matthew 28:18-20.

Church's purpose is three-fold:

1.Make the Gospel known,
2.Make disciples of Christ
3.To make much of Christ.

We make the gospel known through personal evangelism, through preaching the gospel in our local church assembly, and by financially supporting the spread of the gospel worldwide. We make disciples through regular, systematic preaching of the Word of God; through smaller, content and age-based Bible study groups; and through the fellowship and accountability that comes with being part of the body of Christ. make much of Christ through regular worship in psalms, hymns, and spiritual songs. We make much of Christ by living as He commands and humbly showing His love toward others.

Meet our Pastor

Hello! My name is Chad Colburn, and I have served as the pastor of South Liberty since August of 2012. I believe that in our time there is still a need for Bible-believing churches where the Word of God is freshly taught and faithfully followed. We aim to make the gospel known through evangelism inside and outside of our building, to make disciples through organized and organic teaching opportunities, and to make much of the Lord Jesus Christ through worship and service that brings honor and glory to Him. If there is anything we can do to help you, please don’t hesitate to reach out!
